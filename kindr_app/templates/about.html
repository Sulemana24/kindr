About Kindr

Kindness, scaled for everyone

Kindr is a digital crowdfunding platform built to make fundraising simple, transparent, and accessible. We help individuals, communities, churches, and organizations raise funds for healthcare, education, local projects, and causes that matter.

Our mission

In many places, fundraising still relies on informal methods that are stressful, limited in reach, and hard to track. Kindr provides a secure space where campaigns can be created, shared widely, and supported with confidence. We combine technology with community so more people get help when they need it most.

Trust & transparency

We are committed to clear communication: verified accounts, admin review for campaigns where applicable, and tools so donors can follow progress. Your trust is the foundation of everything we build.
